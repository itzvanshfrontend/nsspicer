Our Story

Built on Trust, Quality & Consistency

NS Spicer was founded in Jaipur, Rajasthan, with a simple mission: to connect global buyers with the finest Indian food products through a reliable, transparent, and professionally managed export operation.

Over four decades, we have grown from a regional spice trader into a full-spectrum B2B food exporter — supplying whole spices, ground spices, pickles, rice, Indian snacks, and ready-to-eat products to importers, distributors, and food manufacturers across 15+ countries.

Our Mission

To supply internationally certified Indian food products with complete transparency, consistency, and long-term partnership thinking.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and preferred B2B food export partner for global grocery buyers and food brands.

Our Values

Quality first, honest pricing, reliable documentation, and a relationship-first approach to every client partnership.
